Elden Ring

ELDEN RING is a sprawling open world action RPG developed by From Software. It was directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki, known primarily for his work on the DARK SOULS series. For ELDEN RING, the studio also collaborated with renowned author George R. R. Martin, famous for his "A Song Of Ice And Fire" series of novels, and he is responsible for the worldbuilding and lore.

Following a catastrophic war between the demigods that rule the Lands Between, it falls to a Tarnished warrior to claim the Elden Ring and restore order to the land.
